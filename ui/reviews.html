          We ordered Rumali Roti Masala Papad, Green Apple Mojito, Veg Galouti Kabab, Paneer Tikka Biryani. I must say each of the dish had a very distinct taste.
        Usually Green Apple Mojito is no different from the regular Mojito but here you will get punch of green apple in each sip.

        Veg Galouti Kabab was mainly made up of Mushroom, Potato & Paneer. It was very well cooked and super soft to eat. This is must try starter for the vegetarians.

        Usually when we talk about Veg Biryani it's more about Pulao but this Zaffran breaks that clutter with very well cooked Biryani along with Paneer Tikka Masala in it.

        Overall the food tasted amazing but Zaffran really needs to work on the service. The stewarts need to be more energetic and prompt.

          Good place to go if you are a non-vegetarian. Can't say the same for vegetarians.

        Things that we tried
        1. Paya Shorba
        2. Tomato Dhanoa Shorba
        3. Tandoori Broccoli
        4. Kurmuri Methi Palak Kabab
        5. Murgh Malai Tikka
        6. Mutton Galouti Kabab
        7. Paneer Tikka Masala
        8. Kashmiri Dum Aloo
        9.Chilli Cheese Tandoori Roti
        10. Butter Naan
        11. Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha
        12. Cheese Garlic Naan


        The Good
        1. Rich and tasteful food (mostly true for non-veg)
        2. Good portion
        3. Good ambience
        4. Accessible location

        Things that can be improved
        1. Taste of veg food
        2. A little more extensive menu
        3. Desserts (we had a chocolate shake and a caramel custard and both were just okay)
        4. Garlic Cheese Naan should have been cooked better

        All in all, if you want a good non-veg meal, go to this place. My non-veg friends told me that they liked the food, especially the Matan Shorba. For veg, I suggest have a look at that menu before going and then make up your mind.
